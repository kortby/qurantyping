# Data Deletion Instructions

At QuranTyping, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

## Method 1: Account Deletion (Self-Service)

You can permanently delete your account directly through your profile settings:

1.  **Log in** to your account at [qurantyping.com/login](https://qurantyping.com/login).
2.  Navigate to your **Profile** by clicking on your username or avatar in the top navigation bar.
3.  Select the **Delete Account** tab (indicated by a gear icon ⚙️) from the sidebar menu.
4.  Click the **Delete Account** button.
5.  Confirm your password to verify your identity.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data (including typing history, settings, and personal information) will be **permanently deleted** from our servers immediately. This action cannot be undone.

## Method 2: Contact Support

If you are unable to access your account or prefer to have us handle it for you, you may submit a deletion request via email:

1.  Send an email to [contact@qurantyping.com](mailto:contact@qurantyping.com).
2.  Use the subject line: **Data Deletion Request**.
3.  Please send the email from the address associated with your account so we can verify ownership.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 24-48 hours.

## Facebook / Social Login Data

If you logged in using Facebook or another social provider, deleting your account via Method 1 will also remove the association with that provider and delete the data we received from them.
